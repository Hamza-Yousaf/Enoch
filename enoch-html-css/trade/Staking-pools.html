FAQs for Staking Pools

Ask a Question

1. What are staking pools?

Staking pools let $ENOCH holders to stake their tokens for a certain span of time and earn passive income at the rate of 12 - 16 % APY. The Enoch platform incentivises the long term holders by providing more $ENOCH or $USDC.

2. How much do I earn from staking?

The approximate earnings from each staking pool is provided in the details of the pool. The section which says APY suggests the amount of returns you will receive. For example, if you stake 10,000 $ENOCH in the ENOCH-USDC pool you will receive 1,250 $USDC (12.5% APY) in return over the course of 1 year.

3. Can I withdraw the staked amount anytime?

Yes you would be able to stake and withdraw your tokens at any time. Although, every time you stake a staking fee of 0.5% is charged and when you withdraw, unstaking fee of 1.5% is charged. So if you stake and unstake numerous times, you will end up paying significant amount of tokens in staking and unstaking fees.

4. What is the minimum threshold amount for staking?

There is no minimum threshold to stake $Enoch. You can even stake 1 $ENOCH. You just have to stake enough to cover the staking and unstaking fees assosciated with the pool.

5. How many staking pools are available?

Currently there is only one ENOCH-ENOCH pool open. Soon the ENOCH-USDC pool will be launched where you can stake $Enoch to earn $USDC. There will be even more staking pools launching in the near future which would include staking $Enoch to earn exclusive NFTs and stake NFTs to earn $Enoch.
