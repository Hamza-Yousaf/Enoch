Keno Information

Winning numbers and Keno prize distribution

The rules for playing GG World Keno are very simple; you just have to select the number of numbers you want to play, choosing from a grid numbered from 1 to 70 inclusive.

Select the bet multiplier (between 1 and 10) with the same combination of numbers chosen, and proceed to the payout.

As simple and easy as that, there is a different draw every four minutes 24 hours a day. See in more detail how to play here.

The player can follow the results of the draws and check if his plays have won prizes directly through our platform.

Advantages of playing Keno online

How do I win the jackpot?

In order to win the jackpot you must match all 10 numbers on any of the bets you place, using the x10 multiplier, the maximum possible prize is $1,000,000.

What do I do if I win?

The player can verify winnings through their user account. The money won is deposited directly into your account, and is available for you to reinvest in new purchases of GG World Keno or any of the other lotteries we offer, or to request a withdrawal.

To request a withdrawal, this can be done directly from your user account by clicking on the withdrawal button in the “my transactions” section.

You will then be asked for proof of identity and proof of address and once these documents have been confirmed, you will be able to withdraw your winnings.
